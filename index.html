Personal Legal Services

Personal law is the area of law the deals with matters related to an individual and his or her family. Our practice helps clients address their legal and financial concerns in areas including estate planning, insurance matters, civil suits, criminal matters, asset transfers, education law and other.

Business Law Services

Business law encompasses the law governing contracts, sales, commercial paper, agency and employment law, business organizations, property, and bailments. Business law includes matters related to starting, selling, or buying a business, managing a business, dealing with employees, negotiating contracts, intellectual property, trademarks, and patents.
